Inventory Management

Take advantage of the capability to oversee inventory on a store-by-store basis throughout the entire organization. Easily monitor the product lifecycle, movement, inter-store transfers, stock availability, near expiry dates, and other relevant inventory details in a centralized location. The inclusion of stock adjustment and daily stock check modules adds flexibility to the efficient management of inventory.

Scanner and Scale Integration

Scanner and scale integration find widespread application in grocery stores and self-checkout stations due to their swift and efficient scanning capabilities. They offer the option of an integrated scale to capture item weights. The expansive scanning area allows for the rapid scanning of items without the need to orient or aim directly at the barcode.

Efficient stock balancing

The Smart POS software features an automated and intelligent alert system, providing versatile control over stock availability, maintenance, and demand predictions. The transfer management module assists in maintaining optimal stock quantities by facilitating the transfer of stocks from overstocked branches to understocked branches.

Integration Services

The Smart POS software ensures real-time synchronization of both physical and online inventory during customer transactions. Additionally, the integration with suppliers (B2B) and customers (C2B) serves as added advantages, simplifying the management of extensive data complexities.

Back Office Reporting

Retrieve reports from any location and on any device, thanks to the cloud-based reporting system. This eliminates the necessity of being physically present to review diverse report data. The real-time cloud synchronization ensures rapid access to reports at your fingertips.

Fully integrated planning

The various modules within the Smart POS software collaborate seamlessly to deliver a user-friendly experience, requiring minimal to no training for effective use. Analyzing and reporting diverse data is effortless, and the direct interpretation of data empowers users for integrated business planning with ease.

Real-time visibility

The Smart POS software consistently refreshes transaction, sales, customer, and other data, updating the central database in real-time. This centralized interface facilitates immediate access to the real-time customer database.
